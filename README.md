# Real-Estate-Market-Analytics
A 4-page Power BI report analysing 5,000 EU property listings across 10 countries and 10 major cities — designed as a decision-making tool for real estate investors, analysts and marketplace teams.
📊 Page 1 — Executive Overview: Market at a glance with KPI cards, map and listing trends
💰 Page 2 — Pricing Deep-Dive: What drives property value across the EU
🕐 Page 3 — Market Liquidity: How fast properties sell and where stale listings concentrate
💡 Page 4 — Investor Opportunity Finder: Yield, capital gains and value plays
Key insights uncovered:
🔹 France and Netherlands command prices 3x higher than Poland
🔹 46% of EU listings are stale — sitting unsold for 180+ days
🔹 Belgium leads capital gains at 116.9% appreciation
🔹 Warsaw and Prague are the top investor value plays
